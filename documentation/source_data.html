Introduction to the CTO Coach Directory

Increasingly, there is an offshoot from traditional leadership coaching aimed more directly at CTOs, which we can call CTO Coaching. Many of these coaches (but not all) are ex-CTOs with a tailored set of material and complementary experiences that directly address the challenges faced by CTOs.

This niche has emerged because CTOs are less likely to seek help, less likely to have relevant help available, and have arguably one of the most challenging executive roles today.

As a CTO Coach and co-host of a show dedicated to helping CTOs, I‚Äôve had the privilege of meeting many of my peers. Here‚Äôs a directory of the CTO Coaches I am aware of globally.
